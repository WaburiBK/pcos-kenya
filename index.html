What is PCOS Kenya?

PCOS Kenya is a non-profit organization that seeks to raise the awareness around PCOS as a condition that is affecting many women in Kenya.

What We Do

In addition, the organization seeks to create a community of Cysters that can share their stories and experiences in a bid to encourage one another and provide a safe space for other women. We aim to actualize this through:

  • Sharing Stories and Experiences
  • Healthy and Realistic Meal Plans
  • Sharing Resources on PCOS
  • Organized Meetups

What is PCOS?

PCOS stands for Polycystic Ovarian Syndrome and affects very many women in Kenya.

Common Symptoms

Since PCOS goes undiagnosed in many women, it is important to have a look at whether you share these common symptoms that other women exeperience. These include:

  • Oligovulation (irregular period) or Anovulation (absent period)
  • High levels or resistance to insulin
  • Acne
  • Poor body image
  • Sexual dysfunction
  • Sleep apnea
  • Hidradenitis suppurativa
  • Easy weight gain or obesity
